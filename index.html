BSafe Card

Cold storage startup - March 2021 (ongoing)

One day while trying to find an affordable crypto seed backup to store my Bitcoin mnemonic words, I was so frustrated I could not find one of great quality at a reasonable price. So I decided to make my own, and post it on Reddit. The post blew up in a few hours, so I decided to professionally offer them to other HODLers always having in mind how frustrated I felt myself at the very beginning. This is how this idea also became my Business Management thesis that if you are curious can access here.

Today the product is available on BSafe Card official web where payments in several crypto currencies are accepted. To enable this I used Coinbase API to integrate in my shop. As Coinbase does only provide the payment bridge but does not collect and store customer shipping information I built a paralell system to accomplish this.

The product is also available on Etsy.
